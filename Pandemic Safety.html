Corona Is Still Here



The Safety Measures to be followed during the pandemic are:-


  • The most important thing is to wear a mask. The most protective mask is the N95 Mask, so don't forget to wear a mask in public places.

  • The second thing is to wash hands with soap for atleast 20 seconds, or sanitize hands with a sanitizer. Wash, wash, wash!

  • Keep a distance of atleast 6 feet from everyone.

  • If syptoms like fever, cough, headache, difficulty in breathing or not able to taste or smell occur, isolate immediately and seek medical advice.

  • If your age is above 18, take the Covid-19 vaccine.

  • Prevent going out if not neccessary.
